Job Title: Data Analyst
Department: Business Intelligence & Analytics
Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)
Experience: 2–5 Years

─────────────────────────────────────────
ABOUT THE ROLE
─────────────────────────────────────────
We are looking for a detail-oriented Data Analyst to join our BI team. You will
translate raw business data into actionable insights, build dashboards, and work
closely with product, operations, and finance teams to drive data-informed
decisions. This is NOT a machine learning or data science role — we need someone
who is exceptional at analysis, reporting, and storytelling with data.

─────────────────────────────────────────
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
─────────────────────────────────────────
- Extract, clean, and transform data from multiple sources using SQL and Python
- Build and maintain interactive dashboards in Tableau and Power BI
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify trends, anomalies, and
  business opportunities
- Write complex SQL queries including CTEs, window functions, and subqueries
- Collaborate with stakeholders to define KPIs and metrics frameworks
- Conduct A/B test analysis and interpret statistical significance
- Prepare weekly and monthly business performance reports for leadership
- Maintain data dictionaries and documentation for all reporting assets
- Identify data quality issues and work with data engineering to resolve them
- Present findings clearly to non-technical audiences using data storytelling

─────────────────────────────────────────
REQUIRED SKILLS & QUALIFICATIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────
Technical Skills:
- SQL (Advanced): CTEs, window functions, joins, aggregations, query optimization
- Python: pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn for data manipulation and visualization
- Tableau: dashboard creation, calculated fields, LOD expressions, data blending
- Power BI: DAX formulas, data modeling, report publishing
- Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets: pivot tables, VLOOKUP, advanced formulas
- Data warehousing concepts: star schema, fact/dimension tables
- Experience with at least one cloud data warehouse: BigQuery, Redshift, or Snowflake
- Basic statistics: mean, median, standard deviation, correlation, hypothesis testing

Soft Skills:
- Strong analytical and critical thinking
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Ability to translate complex data into simple business narratives
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Stakeholder management and presentation skills

Education:
- Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science,
  or a related quantitative field

─────────────────────────────────────────
NICE TO HAVE

- Experience with Jira and Confluence for project tracking
