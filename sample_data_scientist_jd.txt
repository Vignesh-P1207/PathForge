Senior Data Scientist

Company: TechCorp Analytics
Location: Remote / Hybrid
Experience: 3-5 years

Job Description:

We are seeking a talented Senior Data Scientist to join our growing analytics team. You will work on cutting-edge machine learning projects, develop predictive models, and drive data-driven decision making across the organization.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and deploy machine learning models for business applications
- Analyze large datasets to extract actionable insights and patterns
- Build and maintain data pipelines for model training and deployment
- Collaborate with engineering teams to productionize ML models
- Conduct A/B testing and statistical analysis to measure impact
- Present findings and recommendations to stakeholders
- Mentor junior data scientists and contribute to team growth

Required Skills & Qualifications:

Technical Skills:
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Experience with Machine Learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn)
- Deep understanding of statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
- Hands-on experience with data manipulation libraries (Pandas, NumPy)
- Knowledge of big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop, or similar)
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Proficiency in data visualization tools (Matplotlib, Seaborn, Tableau, or PowerBI)
- Understanding of MLOps practices and model deployment
- Experience with version control (Git) and collaborative development

Machine Learning Expertise:
- Supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms
- Deep Learning and Neural Networks
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Time series forecasting
- Feature engineering and selection
- Model evaluation and optimization
- A/B testing and experimentation

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
- Experience with Docker and Kubernetes for model deployment
- Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines for ML workflows
- Familiarity with Apache Kafka or similar streaming platforms
- Experience with graph databases (Neo4j) or NoSQL databases (MongoDB)
- Publications or contributions to open-source ML projects
- Experience in specific domains (finance, healthcare, e-commerce)

Soft Skills:
- Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking
- Excellent communication skills for technical and non-technical audiences
- Ability to work independently and in cross-functional teams
- Agile/Scrum methodology experience
- Project management capabilities

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and equity package
- Flexible work arrangements
- Professional development budget
- Health and wellness benefits
- Collaborative and innovative work environment
- Opportunity to work on impactful projects with real-world applications

To Apply:
Submit your resume, portfolio of projects, and a brief cover letter explaining your interest in the role.
